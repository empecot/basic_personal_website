Emily Pecot

DESIGN

From the nomadic life of a military child, through aspirations of becoming a war correspondent, and then the surreal life of cinema production--Emily's draws inspiration from seeing the world, hearing stories and finding life's fleeting moments of transcendence. Inspired by her experiences as a mother, a wanderer and an outsider, her work conveys humility cloaked in style and whimsy.

Specializing in Front End Web Design, Emily seeks to provide functionality, organization and elegant simplicity.

Emily balances her digital life with a healthy dose of music, gardening, bicycling, kickboxing, cooking, playing with her daughter and hiking her property in the Hudson Valley.
